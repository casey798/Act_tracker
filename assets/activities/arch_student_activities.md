# The Architecture Student Matrix: Single Word Concepts

A curated list of 64 activities (16 per category) specific to the studio culture, design process, and lifestyle of architecture students.

---

## 1. High Effort / Social
*Collaborative studio work, juries, and on-site activities.*

1.  **Charretting** (Intense collaborative design sprints)
2.  **Presenting** (Juries/Reviews)
3.  **Surveying** (Site measurement)
4.  **Critiquing** (Giving/receiving feedback)
5.  **Constructing** (1:1 scale installations)
6.  **Debating** (Design theory)
7.  **Exhibiting** (Setting up year-end shows)
8.  **Networking** (Guest lectures/Firm visits)
9.  **Curating** ( organizing panels/work)
10. **Workshoping** (Group model making)
11. **Collaborating** (Group projects)
12. **Defending** (Thesis/Final jury)
13. **Mapping** (Urban analysis groups)
14. **Brainstorming**
15. **Mentoring** (Helping juniors)
16. **Directing** (Managing a team section)

---

## 2. High Effort / Personal
*The solitary grind: drafting, modeling, and technical focus.*

1.  **Drafting** (AutoCAD/Revit)
2.  **Rendering** (Lumion/V-Ray/Twinmotion)
3.  **Modeling** (Physical models)
4.  **Sketching** (Concept development)
5.  **Detailing** (Construction drawings)
6.  **Scripting** (Grasshopper/Parametric)
7.  **Plotting** (Dealing with large format printers)
8.  **Redlining** (Reviewing/Correcting own plans)
9.  **Researching** (Case studies)
10. **Calculating** (Structures/Estimations)
11. **Prototyping** (Laser cutting/3D printing)
12. **Writing** (Dissertation/Reports)
13. **Sectioning** (Drawing rigorous sections)
14. **Photocollaging** (Post-production)
15. **Concepting**
16. **Portfolio-ing** (Compiling work)

---

3.  **Pinning** (Casual wall reviews)
4.  **Roaming** (Site visits/City walks)
5.  **Jamming** (Shared studio playlists)
6.  **Gossiping**
7.  **Observing** (People watching for scale)
8.  **Thrifting** (Scavenging materials)
9.  **Lunching**
10. **Sharing** (Resources/Blocks)
11. **Photowalking**
12. **Visiting** (Other studios)
13. **Commuting**
14. **Browsing** (Library books together)
15. **Meme-ing** (Arch-school humor)
16. **Coffee-ing**

## 4. Low Effort / Personal
*Restorative moments and passive consumption.*

1.  **Waiting** (For renders to finish)
2.  **Sleeping** (Under desk/naps)
3.  **Pinterest-ing** (Mood boarding)
4.  **Staring** (Creative block/Zoning out)
5.  **Listening** (Podcasts/Audiobooks)
6.  **Collecting** (Textures/Assets)
7.  **Organizing** (File management)
8.  **Tracing** (Mindless overlay work)
9.  **Doodling** (Non-project sketching)
10. **Watching** (Tutorials/Documentaries)
11. **Sharpening** (Meditative tool care)
12. **Reflecting**
13. **Walking**
14. **Reading** (Magazines/Blogs)
15. **Sourcing** (Looking for references)
16. **Dreaming**
